Education

2020 - 2024

Bachelor of Technology - NIT NAGALAND

Joining NIT Nagaland's ECE department was a dream come true. The institute has a reputation for its exceptional academic programs, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. As I stepped onto the campus, I was greeted by a vibrant community of students from diverse backgrounds, all driven by a passion for engineering. The institute's serene atmosphere, surrounded by nature's beauty, provided the perfect setting for learning and personal growth.

The ECE curriculum at NIT Nagaland is well-designed, combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications. The faculty members, renowned experts in their fields, constantly encouraged us to think critically, explore innovative ideas, and seek solutions to real-world problems. The coursework covered a wide range of subjects, including electrical circuits, digital electronics, signals and systems, communication engineering, and more. Additionally, the institute's well-equipped laboratories offered hands-on experience with cutting-edge equipment and technology.

    Apart from academics, NIT Nagaland provided a plethora of extracurricular activities that enriched my overall college experience. The institute's various clubs and societies allowed me to pursue my interests outside the classroom, develop leadership skills, and interact with like-minded individuals. I actively participated in technical symposiums, workshops, and hackathons, which broadened my horizons and honed my practical skills.

As I progressed through the years, NIT Nagaland continued to provide opportunities for personal and professional growth. The institute organized guest lectures by industry experts, career guidance sessions, and industrial visits to give us a glimpse into the practical aspects of the field. These initiatives not only enhanced my technical knowledge but also helped me develop a broader understanding of the industry's demands and trends. Now, as I near the end of my academic journey, I look back with a profound sense

Intermendiate Education - Bhashyam IIT-JEE junior college

I am honored to share my educational journey as a student who completed their intermediate education at Bhashyam IIT-JEE Junior College in Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. In the 11th class, I was thrilled to receive a CGPA of 9.9, a testament to my hard work and determination. However, my dedication did not waver, and in the 12th class, I achieved the pinnacle of my academic success, securing a perfect CGPA of 10.

My time at Bhashyam IIT-JEE Junior College was transformative. The college provided a supportive and nurturing environment that fostered intellectual growth and encouraged students to strive for their goals. The faculty members were highly experienced and went above and beyond to ensure that we received a comprehensive education. They conducted regular assessments, provided personalized guidance, and offered extra classes to strengthen our foundational knowledge. The college's well-structured curriculum and emphasis on discipline instilled in us the necessary skills to excel in competitive exams like the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE).

01-06-2023 to 07-07-2023

Embedded Systems internship - Pie Infocomm

During my B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering, I had the wonderful opportunity to undertake an internship at Pie Infocomm Company, where I was immersed in the exciting world of embedded systems. This internship provided me with invaluable hands-on experience in designing, developing, and testing embedded systems for various applications. I was involved in the entire development cycle, from conceptualizing and prototyping to coding and debugging. Working closely with a team of experienced engineers, I gained practical knowledge in programming languages such as C and C++, as well as utilizing hardware platforms like Arduino and Raspberry Pi. This internship at Pie Infocomm Company not only strengthened my technical skills but also taught me the importance of teamwork, effective communication, and problem-solving in a professional setting. It was an enriching experience that solidified my passion for embedded systems and paved the way for a successful career in the field.
